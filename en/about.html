Luan Rodrigues

I photograph because observing the world with attention helps me understand it better.

São Paulo, Brazil. Photography started as a way to slow down — to pay attention to the light, to the framing, to what usually goes unnoticed.

In people, I look for the natural gesture — the smile that happens on its own, the expression of someone immersed in the moment. I photograph without interrupting, because I believe the best images are born when a person is simply who they are.

My work spans landscapes, portraits and personal projects. Careful composition, minimal interference.
