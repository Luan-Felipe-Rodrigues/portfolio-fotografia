About

Luan Rodrigues

I photograph because observing the world with attention helps me understand it better.

São Paulo, Brazil. I started photography driven by curiosity — by the light that transforms a scene, by the moment that never repeats, by beauty in the everyday.

My work spans portraits, events and personal projects. I seek honest images, with careful composition and minimal interference.

Each project is an opportunity to see something familiar with new eyes. I believe the best photographs are born from patience and presence.
